13 Favorite Art Tools

Hi! This is a list of some of my "favorite" art tools. When illustrating, I find these supplies an essential part of my creative expressions.

  • Zebra Mechanicals
  • Drawing Tablets
  • Prismacolor
  • Oil Painting
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Stick Eraser
  • Inkwell
  • Fountain Pen
  • Clear Rulers
  • Clear Clipboard
  • Water Color
  • Easel and Canvas
  • Printing Paper

Zebra Mechanicals

Zebra mechanical penscils are my go to's when sketching new ideas or rough drafts. They're light and easy to erase with a sturdy exterior and comfortable grip.

Drawing Tablets

When working on digital art or 3D modeling, a good drawing tablet is a must have. A drawing tablet is the next best thing to drawing on paper but let me correct myself. With the growing VR technology, VR tools are now becoming the next best thing.

Prismacolor

Prismacolored Pencils and Markers are professional medium for creating high quality artwork.

Oil Paints

Oil Paints take some skill to use but result in detailed, professional, timeless artwork. Oil paints can also be used for creating realistic backgrounds for other projects as well.

Adobe Photoshop

Photoshop is a good way to scan and finalize your art and sometimes even a shortcut for inking and coloring. It gives several advanced options such as blur, blend, magic eraser and even AI generating.
